Chimichurri

Description

This is a simple recipe for a chimichurri sauce. This sauce pairs great with steak and can be prepared with a blender in 5 minutes or less!

Ingredients

  • 1 shallot
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 cup parsley
  • 1/2 cup red wine vinegar
  • 3/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2-3 dried chilis

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients in a bowl and let sit for 2-3 minutes
  2. Toss ingredients into a food processor or blender
  3. Use as a marinade or toppign for steak!
