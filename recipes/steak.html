Ribeye Steak

Description

This is a simple recipe for a ribeye steak. This recipe will be prepared in 4 easy steps and take under 10 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 12oz Ribeye Steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 tbsp of Butter
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Thyme

Steps

  1. Rub dry ingredients on Ribeye
  2. Heat up butter and thyme in a skillet
  3. Cook until steak reaches desired temperature
  4. Allow steak to cool 5 minutes before serving
